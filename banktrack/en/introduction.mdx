[Banktrack](https://banktrack.com) is an online tool to take control of your finances.

You can use it to:

- Connect all your [banks](/banktrack/en/banks/how-to-connect-a-bank) and get a global view of your finances.
- Share financial information with whomever you want without needing to share your banking credentials, and send it through the [channels](/banktrack/en/integrations/how-to-connect-a-channel) you prefer (WhatsApp, email, Telegram, SMS).
- Create periodic [reports](/banktrack/en/reports/how-to-create-a-custom-report) of your finances with all the information you need: transactions, balances, trend graphs, tables, etc.
- Set up custom [alerts](/banktrack/en/alerts/how-to-create-a-custom-alert) to instantly know what's happening with your banks.
- Create [automatic rules](/banktrack/en/automations/how-to-create-an-automation) that allow you to organize your banking transactions easily and without manual work (categorize, label, modify transaction descriptions, etc.).
- Visualize your finances on personalized [dashboards](/banktrack/en/dashboards/how-to-create-a-custom-dashboard) that automatically update with your bank information.
- [Search](/banktrack/en/transactions/how-to-use-the-transaction-finder) transactions across all your accounts in one click, just like you would on Google.
- Automatically [reconcile invoices](/banktrack/en/invoices/how-to-reconcile-invoices) with transactions.
- Create [forecasts](/banktrack/en/forecasts/how-to-make-a-future-forecast) of what will happen in your accounts in the coming months.

- **SMEs:** Keep track of your company's finances easily and without wasting time on complex tools or spreadsheets.
