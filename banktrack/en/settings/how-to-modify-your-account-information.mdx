---
title: "How to modify your account information"
description: "Edit your profile and account data in Banktrack."
---

In this section, you can edit:

- **First and Last Name.**
- **Banktrack Login Email.**
- **Google Account Linkage:** To access Banktrack with Google.
- **Language:** Currently available only in Spanish.
- **Quiet Hours:** During this time, you will not receive alerts. If an alert occurs during quiet hours, it will be sent once the quiet period ends.
- **Delete Your Banktrack Account.**
