## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Follow these steps to resolve frequent problems quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Connection" icon="wifi">
    Verify your internet connection and refresh the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cache" icon="trash-2">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Browser" icon="globe">
        Press `{Ctrl}`+`{Shift}`+`{R}` (Windows) or `{Cmd}`+`{Shift}`+`{R}` (macOS).
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="App" icon="smartphone">
        Force close and restart the Banktrack mobile app.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Bank Sync" icon="refresh-cw">
    Reconnect your bank account in Settings > Integrations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support" icon="mail">
    If issues persist, reach out via the help widget.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="alert">
  For transaction sync delays `{>24}` hours, check your bank's API status.
</Callout>
