## Overview

Banktrack provides real-time tracking of your business finances across expenses and income. You categorize transactions automatically and access reports via email, WhatsApp, or your dashboard. Start your free trial today to manage treasury without external accountants.

<Callout kind="tip">
No credit card required for the 14-day free trial. Upgrade anytime for full features used by over 1000 Spanish companies.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Banktrack" icon="globe">
    Go to [app.banktrack.es](https://app.banktrack.es) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your business email, company name, and create a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link and click it to activate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect Your First Data Source

Link your bank account via secure Open Banking APIs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Provider" icon="database">
    Select your Spanish bank (e.g., BBVA, Santander) from the integrations list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access" icon="shield">
    Follow the redirect to grant read-only permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Webhook (Optional)" icon="plug">
    For real-time notifications, add a webhook endpoint.

    <CodeGroup tabs="Node.js,Python">
```javascript
const express = require('express');
const app = express();

app.post('/banktrack-webhook', express.raw({type: 'application/json'}), (req, res) => {
  console.log('New transaction:', req.body);
  res.status(200).send('OK');
});

app.listen(3000, () => console.log('Webhook ready'));
```
```python
from flask import Flask, request

app = Flask(__name__)

@app.route('/banktrack-webhook', methods=['POST'])
def webhook():
    data = request.data
    print('New transaction:', data)
    return 'OK', 200

if __name__ == '__main__':
    app.run(port=3000)
```
    </CodeGroup>

    Paste `https://your-domain.com/banktrack-webhook` into Banktrack settings as `{WEBHOOK_URL}`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
