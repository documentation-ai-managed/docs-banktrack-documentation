---
title: "How to send invoices to Banktrack"
description: "Learn the different ways to upload invoices to Banktrack."
---

To reconcile your invoices in Banktrack, you need to upload them to your dashboard. You can do this in several ways:

![Upload invoice to Banktrack](https://content.gitbook.com/content/IhgipbuJxCqyX8vQOfan/blobs/nHmK7cAiGjoj57DQEx40/iScreen%20Shoter%20-%20Google%20Chrome%20-%20240220143813.png)

- Dragging the invoices into the Banktrack space or clicking on Add Invoice.
- Sending them by email to the personalized email address provided to you.
- Sending them to Banktrack's WhatsApp.
- Sending them to Banktrack's Telegram.
