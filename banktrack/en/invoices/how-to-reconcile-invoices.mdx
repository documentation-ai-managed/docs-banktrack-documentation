In Banktrack, you can store all your invoices (both income and expense) and easily link them to your bank transactions. This will provide greater traceability and simplified management with your accounting service.

Banktrack allows you to:

- Send your invoices to a personalized email address, via WhatsApp, or Telegram to view them directly in your account.
- Scan the invoice and automatically extract the data without any manual work.
- Associate invoices with Customers and Suppliers from your Contact list.
- Reconcile the invoice with bank transactions in one click.

If you have any questions regarding the Payments and Collections section, you can contact us via the chat you'll find in the bottom right corner of the screen.
