---
title: "How to add additional users"
description: "Add additional users to your Banktrack account."
---

From this section, you can add additional users to your Banktrack account.

Steps to Follow:

1. Go to **Settings**.
2. Select the **Users** section.
3. Click on **+ Add** and enter the email of the person you want to invite to collaborate on your account.

<Callout kind="info">
  **Note:** Only the account owner has permission to add additional users.
</Callout>

  **User Limitations:** The number of users you can add depends on the plan you have subscribed to. You can check this in the **Subscription** section.
</Callout>
