---
title: "How to connect a channel"
description: "Connect channels like email, WhatsApp, Telegram, SMS, and Slack to receive reports and alerts."
---

To connect a new channel in Banktrack, such as email, WhatsApp, or Telegram, the first thing you need to do is go to the [Channels](https://app.banktrack.com/integraciones) section.

Click on the **Add channel** button to select your preferred channel. You can add as many channels as you like. Below are the details for adding each of the channels:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="WhatsApp">
    Enter the WhatsApp number along with the country code and click **Continue**.

    You will receive a code on your WhatsApp number that you need to enter on the next screen to validate that the number is correct. After this, click **Add WhatsApp** to create the channel.

    ![Connect WhatsApp](https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-54c75dd1-b435-4e90-af26-bd7d8846081b/doc-3ced55c8-092e-4386-b871-aab18b35003e/1767001071220-llorc6o50r8-image.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat)

    <Callout kind="alert">
      If you do not receive the verification code on your mobile, it's possible that **you haven't accepted WhatsApp's Terms and Privacy Policies**. We recommend **adding the Banktrack bot** (+34 629 95 59 67) **and sending it a message**. It will respond with a link that you need to open on your device to **accept the update.** If you do not accept it, conversations with businesses cannot be initiated.
    </Callout>

    ![Validate WhatsApp](https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-54c75dd1-b435-4e90-af26-bd7d8846081b/doc-3ced55c8-092e-4386-b871-aab18b35003e/1767001172466-czf4dkp3oso-image.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat)

    Once the connection is complete, if you already have reports and alerts created, you can link the newly added channel to your existing reports and alerts.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Email">
    Enter the email address and click **Continue**.

    ![Connect email](https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-54c75dd1-b435-4e90-af26-bd7d8846081b/doc-3ced55c8-092e-4386-b871-aab18b35003e/1767001057773-0gsx4i8o4ytv-image.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat)

    You will receive a code in the email that you need to enter on the next screen to validate that the address is correct. After this, click **Add Email** to create the channel.

    <Callout kind="alert">
      If you do not receive the verification code, check your SPAM or Promotions folder. If you still encounter issues, you can contact us via the chat you'll find in the bottom right corner of the screen.
    </Callout>

    ![Validate email](https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-54c75dd1-b435-4e90-af26-bd7d8846081b/doc-3ced55c8-092e-4386-b871-aab18b35003e/1767001087980-4bw35ibugme-image.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat)

    Once the connection is complete, if you already have reports and alerts created, you can link the newly added channel to your existing reports and alerts.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Telegram">
    Scan the QR code with your mobile camera or QR scanner and click **Open Telegram** on the screen you see.

    When you are redirected to the Telegram app, click the **Start button** in the Telegram bot.

    You will receive a code in the chat that you need to enter on the next screen to validate that the number is correct. After this, click **Add Telegram** to create the channel.

    ![Connect Telegram](https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-54c75dd1-b435-4e90-af26-bd7d8846081b/doc-3ced55c8-092e-4386-b871-aab18b35003e/1767001061561-v3clf40w7e7-image.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat)

    Once the connection is complete, if you already have reports and alerts created, you can link the newly added channel to your existing reports and alerts.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="SMS">
    Enter the number along with the country code and click **Continue**.

    ![Connect SMS](https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-54c75dd1-b435-4e90-af26-bd7d8846081b/doc-3ced55c8-092e-4386-b871-aab18b35003e/1767001091579-fvquy3r4qjs-image.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat)

    You will receive a code on your mobile number that you need to enter on the next screen to validate that the number is correct. After this, click **Add SMS** to create the channel.

    <Callout kind="alert">
      If you do not receive the verification code on your mobile, you can contact us via the chat you'll find in the bottom right corner of the screen.
    </Callout>

    ![Validate SMS](https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-54c75dd1-b435-4e90-af26-bd7d8846081b/doc-3ced55c8-092e-4386-b871-aab18b35003e/1767001141430-djinjlofp9-image.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat)

    Once the connection is complete, if you already have reports and alerts created, you can link the newly added channel to your existing reports and alerts.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Slack">
    Click the "Link Slack" button and follow the steps on the Slack page.

    ![Connect Slack](https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-54c75dd1-b435-4e90-af26-bd7d8846081b/doc-3ced55c8-092e-4386-b871-aab18b35003e/1767001089618-dvlp0m8nxar-image.png?q=85&fm=auto&auto=compress%2Cformat)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

If you have any questions about connecting a channel, you can contact us via the chat you'll find in the bottom right corner of the screen.
