---
title: "How to modify billing information"
description: "Update your Banktrack account billing information."
---

In this section, you can edit the following:

- **Company Name:** The name that will appear on your invoice.
- **Country.**
- **Tax ID (NIF/CIF).**
- **Billing Email.**
- **Billing Phone Number.**
- **Company's Postal Address.**
