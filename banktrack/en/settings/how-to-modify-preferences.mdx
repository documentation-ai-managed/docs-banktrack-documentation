---
title: "How to modify preferences"
description: "Configure your Banktrack account preferences."
---

In this section, you can edit the following:

- **Project Name:** The name that appears on your profile button.
- **Default Country.**
- **Default Currency:** We will use the currency defined here to calculate total balances, but each transaction will be associated with its original currency.
- **Time Zone:** To receive the periodic report at the time adjusted to the time zone you are in.
