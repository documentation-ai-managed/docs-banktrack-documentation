---
title: "How to manage my Banktrack subscription"
description: "Manage your subscription, change plans, or download invoices."
---

In this section, you can:

- View the plan you are subscribed to.
- Check the plan renewal date.
- Explore additional plans and pricing.
- Manage your subscription (change or cancel the plan).
- Download the invoices issued for your subscription.

To access this section, follow these steps:

![How to access Settings](https://content.gitbook.com/content/IhgipbuJxCqyX8vQOfan/blobs/3jHmUCJFbAcxWt8A7QK9/Ajustes%20acceso.gif)

Then select the **Subscription** section:

![Manage Banktrack subscription](https://content.gitbook.com/content/IhgipbuJxCqyX8vQOfan/blobs/Az1a3EKpxjbCG4QQQyYd/CleanShot%202024-04-11%20at%2011.11.27@2x.png)

By clicking the **Manage Subscription** button, you will be directed to the Stripe panel (the platform we use for payment and billing) where you can modify your plan or download previous invoices.

If you have any questions about your subscription or billing, you can contact us through the chat located in the bottom right corner of the screen.
